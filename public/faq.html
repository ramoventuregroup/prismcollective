PrismCollective is a sustainable lifestyle brand offering premium eco-friendly essentials for your home and pets. We curate high-quality products made from natural, renewable, and responsibly sourced materials.

We work with carefully selected manufacturers across multiple countries who share our commitment to quality materials and ethical production. Each product page includes specific material and care details.

Standard shipping typically takes 7–12 business days depending on your location. We're working to offer faster shipping options in the future.

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase, contact us and we'll make it right. Depending on the product, returns may be handled directly with our fulfillment partners.

Email us at hello@prismcollective.us and we'll respond within 24 hours.

We prioritize products made from natural, renewable, and responsibly sourced materials like bamboo, organic cotton, natural rubber, and recycled materials. We're transparent about materials and origin — no greenwashing, just honest products.

Currently we ship within the United States. International shipping options are coming soon.
